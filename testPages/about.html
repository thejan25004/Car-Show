OUR STORY

AUTOLUX EXPERIENCE

Founded in 2010, AUTOLUX has been at the forefront of luxury automotive experiences for over 15 years. Our mission is to provide enthusiasts and collectors with unparalleled access to the world's most exclusive high-performance vehicles.

Our team of automotive experts and passionate designers works tirelessly to curate a collection that represents the pinnacle of engineering excellence and aesthetic perfection. We believe that a luxury vehicle is more than transportation—it's an expression of ambition, creativity, and technological mastery.

From our state-of-the-art showrooms to our immersive online platform, we're committed to creating automotive experiences that inspire and delight our clients. Every AUTOLUX customer receives personalized attention and expert guidance throughout their journey with us.

OUR JOURNEY

2010

AUTOLUX Founded

Founded by Alexander Wright with a vision to revolutionize the luxury automotive experience.

2013

First Flagship Showroom

Opened our first state-of-the-art showroom in Los Angeles, setting new standards for automotive retail.

2015

International Expansion

Expanded operations to Europe with showrooms in London and Monaco, bringing the AUTOLUX experience to international clients.

2018

Custom Experience Program

Launched our exclusive customization program, allowing clients to collaborate with our designers on bespoke vehicles.

2021

Sustainable Luxury Initiative

Introduced our commitment to sustainable luxury with electric and hybrid performance vehicles and eco-friendly showroom practices.

2025

Digital Experience Platform

Launched our immersive digital platform, bringing the AUTOLUX experience to enthusiasts worldwide with virtual showrooms and AR technology.
